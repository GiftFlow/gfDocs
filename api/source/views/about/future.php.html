The Future

		
		
We have wonderful plans for the future of GiftFlow. From uploading
		photos to a revenue model, there is much in store. 

		
		
Verified Donations

		
One of our first planned improvements is the addition of a Verified Donation system. 
		You will have the option of becoming Verified by making a $20 donation with your
		credit card or via PayPal. This gives us the ability to verify your real name. In return,
		you'll recieve a "Verified" badge on your profile.

		
		
Our goal for the system is twofold. We expect that it will provide an additional level
		of credibility, making our community safer and more trustworthy. We also hope to 
		cover all of GiftFlow's operating costs. As a non-profit, 
		our goal is to keep the site as un-monetized as possible.

		
		
What do you want to see?

		
If you have an idea for how to make GiftFlow a better website and community, let us know!
		Click on the Feedback tab on the left-hand side of your window.

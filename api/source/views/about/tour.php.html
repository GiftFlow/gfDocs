The Tour

		
Welcome to GiftFlow! The site is currently a work in progress. At the moment, we are in the Alpha stage of development. This means a lot is happening, and you should stay tuned for lots of cool new stuff.

		
Get Free Stuff

		
GiftFlow makes it easy for you to find anything you need in your area. For free. Search for others' gifts by keywords, tags and location. List your needs and the site will automatically pair you with matching gifts nearby.

		
Reuse Your Stuff

		
Got some stuff you don't want any more? GiftFlow makes it easy to reuse it by connecting you with people nearby who need what you have to give.

		
Build a Reputation for Generosity

		
Your GiftFlow profile lists everything you've given and everything you've taken. Profiles enable trust to develop between members, which in turn enables the online GiftFlow community to come to being in the real world.

GiftFlow is a community where you can get involved by getting things you need for free and finding people who need the stuff you have to give away. GiftFlow connects community organizations, businesses, governments and neighbors into a giant network of reciprocity, where they can share resources, meet each others' needs and coordinate their efforts to build a better world. GiftFlow is here so that people can work cooperatively without the need for money.
